📀 What is Magnetic Storage?

Magnetic storage saves data by magnetising tiny areas on a disk surface. The read/write head moves across the spinning platter to access different tracks.

  • Hard Disk Drives (HDDs)
  • Magnetic Tape Drives
  • Floppy Disks (historical)

✅ Benefits

  • Cheaper per GB (£0.02-0.05/GB)
  • High capacity (up to 20TB+)
  • Good for long-term storage
  • Reliable for many read/write cycles

❌ Drawbacks

  • Moving parts = fragile
  • Slower than SSDs (80-160 MB/s)
  • Noisy operation
  • Not ideal for portable devices
