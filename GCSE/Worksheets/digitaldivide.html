Causes and Solutions

What Causes the Divide?

  • Cost: High prices for devices and internet plans.
  • Infrastructure: Lack of fibre broadband or mobile signal in rural or remote areas.
  • Skills & Confidence: Lack of training or fear of technology, especially among older populations.
  • Disability: Websites or software not being accessible to users with disabilities.

How Can We Reduce the Divide?

  • Government Investment: Funding broadband rollout to rural areas.
  • Public Access: Providing free Wi-Fi and computer access in libraries and community centres.
  • Training Programs: Offering free courses to improve digital literacy and skills for all ages.
  • Accessible Design: Ensuring technology is designed to be usable by people with disabilities.
