🎯 What's Happening Now?

Click on any two devices to see how PSTN creates a dedicated circuit between them. The network will find a path through the switches!

📡 Physical Media Types

  • Telephone Lines: Traditional copper wires
  • Fiber Optics: High-speed light transmission
  • Cellular Networks: Wireless radio waves
  • Satellites: Long-distance communication
  • Undersea Cables: International connections

🔀 Circuit Switching

A dedicated path is established between two devices for the entire duration of communication. This path remains exclusive to the communicating parties until the connection ends.

💡 Key Concepts

  • 📌 Switch: Routes your call
  • 📌 Circuit: Dedicated path
  • 📌 Exclusive: No sharing during call
  • 📌 Released: Resources freed after call

✅ Benefits of Dedicated Lines

  • Faster connection and data transmission
  • More consistent transmission speeds
  • Improved security - exclusive use
  • No competition for bandwidth
  • Lower latency for critical applications

❌ Drawbacks of Dedicated Lines

  • Expensive to set up and maintain
  • No alternative if line fails
  • Inflexible - fixed capacity
  • Underutilized during low traffic
  • Installation causes disruption
